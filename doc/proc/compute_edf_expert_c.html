subroutine compute_edf_expert_c(values, n_values, perm, unique_values, cdf_values, n_unique, ierr) bind(C, name="compute_edf_expert_c")

Uses

    • f42_utils
    • iso_fortran_env
    • iso_c_binding

Expert C wrapper for compute_edf. Direct interface to compute_edf for users who have already sorted their data or have a custom permutation vector. This skips the internal sorting step for better performance when the caller has full control.
