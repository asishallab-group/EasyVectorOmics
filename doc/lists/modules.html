tox_data_integration tox_data_integration.F90

In multi-study omics analyses, it is often unclear whether biological replicates originating from different studies can be safely treated as sampling the same biological condition. Even when studies nominally target the same tissue and condition, differences in sample handling, sequencing technologies, preprocessing pipelines, or cohort, composition can introduce batch effects that are not easily detectable from mean expression levels alone.
