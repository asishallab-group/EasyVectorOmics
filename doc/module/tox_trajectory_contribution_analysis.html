public pure subroutine compute_p_values(local_contributions_observed, total_contribution_observed, local_contributions_perm, total_contributions_perm, n_timepoints, n_permutations, local_p_values, total_p_value, ierr)

Once the permutation tests are calculated (perform_permutation_test), this subroutine calculates the p values for the contributions, i.e. how many of the permutation contributions were at least as high as the real contributions.

number of permutations to perform
